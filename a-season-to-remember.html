A SEASON TO REMEMBER

Category : Shows
Air Date : May 15, 2019
TRT : 22:11
Role : Producer

SEATTLE - Sue Bird and the Seattle Storm are getting set to defend their third WNBA title with the 2019 season is right around the corner. However, they'll have to do it without the 2018 league MVP Breanna Stewart. Aaron Levine and Elise Woodward take a look back at the Storm's 2018 run to the championship and look forward to if Seattle can repeat as WNBA champions.
