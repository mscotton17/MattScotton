About

As the self-titled "Swiss Army Knife" of the KVAL Sports team, I feel at home on the fields of collegiate and high school sports as well as in the booth preparing video segments and managing a webcast.


While my day-to-day includes talking with players after practice, shooting games, and producing shows, my real passion is telling people’s stories. Whether it’s a kid from Eugene who grew up to play on a local semi-pro soccer team or brothers starting for the same D1 football team, everyone has a unique story; you just have to find it.
