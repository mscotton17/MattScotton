TALKIN’ HUSKIES

Category : Shows
Air Date : October 18, 2019
TRT : 23:12
Role : Producer

SEATTLE - The Huskies are looking for revenge after falling to the Ducks last season, 30-27 in overtime. This year’s edition of the storied I-5 rivalry features a duel between two future first-round quarterbacks. Aaron Levine and Terry Hollimon discuss what it will take for Washington to upset #12 Oregon on Montlake.
