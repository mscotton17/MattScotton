UW vs OSU

Category : Shooting
Date : September 30, 2017
TRT : 2:16
Role : Photographer

EUGENE, Ore. - The Oregon State Beavers did not get off to the start they were hoping for in the 2016 season. After a disastrous 1-2 non-conference slate, a 29 point loss in Pullman, and an injury to their starting quarterback for the 3rd straight season the Beavers got a much-needed bye week. But not even a week off could prepare Oregon State for the sixth-ranked Huskies.
